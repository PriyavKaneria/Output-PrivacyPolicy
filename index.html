Privacy-First Approach: Productivity Tracker is designed to keep all your personal data completely private and secure on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to external servers.

1. Information We Access

To provide you with accurate productivity insights, our app requires access to certain device functions. Here's exactly what we access and why:

Usage Stats Permission (Android Usage Access)
Purpose: To analyze your app usage patterns, screen time, and phone pickup frequency.
Data Used: App usage duration, screen on/off times, app launch counts.
Storage: All data is processed and stored locally on your device only.
Notification Permission
Purpose: To send you optional productivity reminders and insights.
Control: You can fully customize or disable all notifications in the app settings.
Content: Only productivity-related reminders - no promotional content.

2. How We Process Your Data

Local Processing Only

  • Screen Time Analysis: Your usage stats are analyzed locally to calculate productive time
  • Sleep Pattern Detection: Phone inactivity periods are processed to estimate sleep patterns
  • Activity Tracking: Manual activity entries are stored in your device's local database
  • Trend Analysis: Historical data patterns are computed on-device for insights

‚úÖ What We DON'T Do

  • Send your data to any external servers or cloud services
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Track your location or personal identity
  • Access your personal files, photos, or contacts
  • Monitor your communications or browsing history
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All your productivity data is stored exclusively in your device's secure local storage using Android's built-in security mechanisms:

  • Encrypted Storage: Data is stored using Android's secure database encryption
  • App Sandbox: Your data is isolated within the app's private storage space
  • No Cloud Sync: Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it

Data Retention

Your productivity data remains on your device until:

  • You manually delete specific entries through the app
  • You clear the app's data through Android settings
  • You uninstall the application

4. Your Privacy Controls

Notification Settings

You have complete control over notifications:

  • Enable or disable productivity reminders
  • Customize notification frequency and timing
  • Choose which types of insights you want to receive
  • Turn off all notifications while keeping tracking active

Data Management

Through the app settings, you can:

  • View exactly what data is stored
  • Delete specific time periods or activity types
  • Export your data for personal backup
  • Reset all data to start fresh
